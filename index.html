About Us

Learn more about our mission to reduce food waste and help communities

Our Mission

The Food Waste Reduction System is dedicated to creating a sustainable future by connecting surplus food with those who need it most. We believe that no food should go to waste when there are people in our communities facing hunger.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, our platform has helped redistribute over 500,000 meals to families in need. What started as a small local initiative has grown into a nationwide network of donors, volunteers, and recipient organizations working together to combat food waste and food insecurity.

Our Vision

We envision a world where food waste is eliminated, and everyone has access to nutritious food. Through technology and community engagement, we're making this vision a reality one meal at a time.
